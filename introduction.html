Background

Based on the paper by Erez Manor and Shlomo Greenberg. Link to Paper.

The AI world is developing rapidly and is used, among other things, to improve and accelerate computers.

Objective

We aim to accelerate IoT devices with limited resources using neural networks. The demonstration uses Ultra96-V2.

Results

Experimental results show a 724x performance improvement compared to software-only implementations. For example, CIFAR-10 image classification time decreased from 20 seconds to 37 milliseconds.
