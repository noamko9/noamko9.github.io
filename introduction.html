Background

This project is based on the work of Erez Manor and Shlomo Greenberg. Read the original paper here.

The AI world is advancing quickly and is being used in various applications to enhance and speed up computing tasks.

Objective

The goal of this project is to accelerate IoT devices with limited resources using neural networks. The project specifically uses the Ultra96-V2 platform.

Results

Results from our experiments show a significant improvement in performance—724 times faster—compared to traditional software implementations. For instance, the classification of CIFAR-10 images was reduced from 20 seconds to 37 milliseconds.
